# Onboarding

Onboarding helps a new customer tenant move from invitation to first useful Annona workflow.

## What onboarding covers

A customer onboarding path should help you:

- sign in through your organization's access flow
- confirm you are in the correct tenant
- understand what data Annona needs first
- upload an initial file or bundle when required
- wait for readiness checks to finish
- open the relevant dataset, agent, or trace view

## Recommended order

1. Accept your invitation or sign in through the tenant access flow.
2. Open Home and review readiness guidance.
3. Upload the first dataset or bundle if prompted.
4. Confirm the dataset appears in Datasets.
5. Ask an Agent a question grounded in that dataset.
6. Review Traces when you need evidence or troubleshooting context.

## Current status language

If an onboarding step is not live end to end in your tenant, Annona should say so plainly in the product. Help content should explain the intended customer workflow without exposing internal delivery or operator procedures.

## Stable help target

Use `/onboarding/` for in-product help from onboarding cards, setup tasks, readiness banners, and first-run guidance.
