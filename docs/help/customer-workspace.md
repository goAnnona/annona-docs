# Customer workspace

Your Annona tenant is your organization's customer workspace.

This page explains what customer users should expect inside that workspace and what should stay outside it.

## What belongs in your workspace

A customer workspace should contain the things your organization needs to use Annona directly, including:

- your customer home and onboarding flow
- your uploads and dataset views
- your agent workspaces and grounded answers
- your tenant-scoped traces and run history
- your customer-facing help and support links

## What should stay outside your workspace

Your customer workspace should not expose internal Annona admin surfaces such as:

- provisioning or managing other customers
- platform-wide deployment status
- operator-only runbooks or private proof artifacts
- internal architecture notes or engineering delivery checklists
- traces, datasets, or uploads that belong to another customer

## How to tell you are in the right place

You should be able to identify:

- your organization's tenant name or URL
- customer navigation for Home, Uploads, Agents, Datasets, Traces, and Help
- customer-safe status and error messages
- links that open customer docs instead of internal engineering material

## If something looks wrong

If you see internal-only material in the customer product or customer docs, treat that as a product issue and contact your Annona support contact.

If you cannot tell which tenant you are in, or if data appears outside your expected scope, stop and ask for help before continuing.
